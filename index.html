Induction Proof Template

  • 1
    State the predicate $P(n)$ clearly.
  • 2
    Basis Step: Verify $P(n_0)$ directly.
  • 3
    Inductive Hypothesis: Assume $P(k)$ holds for some $k \geq n_0$.
  • 4
    What to Show: State that you must prove $P(k+1)$.
  • 5
    Inductive Step: Use the hypothesis to derive $P(k+1)$.
  • 6
    Conclusion: By induction, $P(n)$ holds for all $n \geq n_0$. $\blacksquare$
